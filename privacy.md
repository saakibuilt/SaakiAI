# Privacy Policy

Last updated: **December 26, 2025**

This Privacy Policy explains how **Saaki A.I.** (“we”, “us”, “our”) collects, uses, shares, and protects information when you use our mobile application and related services (the “App”).

By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide
We may collect information you provide directly, such as:
- **Account information** (e.g., email address, username)
- **Support requests and feedback** (messages you send through the in-app contact form)
- **User-submitted content** such as **chart images** you upload for analysis

### 1.2 Information From Connected Services
If you choose to connect third-party services (for example, **Coinbase**), we may access information from that service as permitted by you and the service provider. This may include:
- Account identifiers necessary to maintain the connection
- Portfolio or balance data used to display your holdings
- Trading-related data required for features you enable (e.g., analytics, signals, or automation)

> **Note:** Connections are optional. You can disconnect at any time in the App.

### 1.3 Automatically Collected Information
We may collect certain information automatically, including:
- Device and app information (device model, OS version, app version)
- App usage events (e.g., screens viewed, feature interactions)
- Diagnostic data (crash logs, performance metrics)

## 2. How We Use Information

We use information to:
- Create and manage your account
- Provide and operate App features (analysis, signals, and connected account functionality)
- Perform **AI-based chart and market analysis** and generate trade insights
- Improve reliability, performance, and user experience
- Communicate with you about support requests and important notices
- Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, or security issues
- Comply with legal obligations

## 3. AI Analysis and User Content

If you upload a **chart image**, the App may process the image to:
- Detect market structure and patterns
- Identify potential levels (support/resistance)
- Provide summaries and suggested setups

We only use your uploaded content to provide the requested functionality and improve service quality.
