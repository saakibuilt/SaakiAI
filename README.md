## 🤖 AI Auto Trading with Coinbase

**Saaki A.I.** is an advanced AI-powered trading system designed to automate cryptocurrency trading through the Coinbase platform. It combines real-time data analysis, intelligent decision-making, and adaptive strategies to execute trades efficiently.

---

## 🚀 Core Features

### 🔗 1. AI Auto Trading (Buy • Sell • Trail)

Saaki A.I. connects directly to Coinbase using secure API access and performs automated trading actions:

- 🤖 **Auto Buy & Sell** based on AI-driven signals  
- 📉 **Trailing strategies** to lock in profits while markets move  
- ⚡ Executes trades in real-time without manual input  
- 📊 Dynamically adjusts positions based on market behavior  

---

### 🧪 2. Backtesting Engine

Test strategies before risking real money.

- ⏪ Run strategies on historical market data  
- 📈 Evaluate performance, win rate, and drawdowns  
- 🧠 AI learns from past patterns to refine decisions  
- ⚙️ Optimize parameters for better accuracy  

---

### 📡 3. AI Signal Generator

Generate trading signals using AI + custom logic.

- 🧠 Create signals using **custom prompts** or indicators  
- 📊 Combine technical indicators (RSI, MACD, etc.) with AI  
- 🔔 Get real-time buy/sell signals  
- 🎯 Adaptive signals based on changing market conditions  

---

### 🔍 4. Smart Chart Scanning

Automatically scan the market and detect opportunities.

- 📈 Analyze multiple charts in real-time  
- 🔎 Detect trends, breakouts, reversals  
- 🤖 AI decides whether to **buy, sell, or wait**  
- ⚡ Continuous scanning without human effort  

---

## ⚙️ How It Works

Saaki A.I. continuously processes:

- Market trends and momentum  
- Price action and volatility  
- Historical patterns  
- AI-driven predictions  

Then executes the best possible trading decision instantly.
